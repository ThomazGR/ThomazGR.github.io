Welcome :)

I am a Sports Scientist that have been studying metabolic disorders, epigenetics, metabolic programming and bioinformatics. I have enormous interest in learning about microRNAs modulation, terapeutic targets, liver disease (mainly NAFLD) and applying + developing bioinformatician methods to discover target(s) genes and/or microRNAs.

Nowadays

Currently I am a MSc student in Nutritional Sciences and Metabolism at Faculty of Applied Sciences at the University of Campinas (UNICAMP). During my Masters degree I am part of the Laboratory of Metabolic Disorders, focused mostly on targets for liver disease and it's potential on a terapeutic aspect within the development of NAFLD or the disease's progression. I use and develop multiple bioinformatics tools to have a greater result and a minimum predition before spending multiple resources on bio mol analysis.

Almost everything is developed in Python 3.6+ using multiple libraries such as Pandas (0.25.3), numpy (1.18), seaborn (0.10) and SciPy (1.4.1). Multiple tools are also used as GEO/GEO2R, limma (R package transformed to Py), TargetScan, miRWalk, KEGG, GO (Gene Ontology) and others.
